3 days ago...

A young lady stands in her resprite block, completely unaware of the misfortune she will find herself in during the next coming days.

It was almost 8 sweeps ago she was given life. It was also almost 8 sweeps ago she was also given a name, because going 8 sweeps without a name is just stupid and will spare the reader of the customary naming ceremony that usually entails in these fictions.

She also has no idea what any of that meant.

Let's find out this young lady's name, shall we?
