Height: 4'8"~ 142cm
Race: Cerulean Alternian

With skills in biotech as well as their experience in Alternian military and sea warfare as a retired lieutenant, Chever makes a difficult adversary to arrest.

Having employed Chever under my jurisdiction in both my personal armada and later my correctional facility, I know first hand of their resourcefulness in overcoming their shortcomings.

Their skills in leadership are hindered by irrationality and grub-like whimsy, making them prone to hasty decisions guided by whims rather than wisdom. Perhaps that is how they lost their entire platoon four sweeps ago, after which they came groveling to me, hoping to escape reprimand.

I took plenty advantage of that.

Derrek Xarres

Allias: Crimson Fox
Age: 14 sweeps
Gender: Male
Height: 5'4" ~ 162cm
Race: Rust Alternian

I had raised Derrek for roughly ten sweeps honing him like a fine edge. After thousands of hours, through strict regime, he has now become a highly skilled swordsman and quite an effective killing machine.

At least, he would be effective if he would keep his head out of the clouds. Derrek's tendency to let his emotions overrule his thoughts often causes him to falter, despite my corrections. I am hoping his recent treason is part of a grander scheme.

He has shown signs to be a gifted psiionic, able to tap into souls, however he failed miserably under my watch. Though, I have been hearing possible reports that he was able to succeed recently.

This concerns me.
